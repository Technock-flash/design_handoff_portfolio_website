04 — How I Work

A quiet,
deliberate process.

Discover → Deliver
Step 01

Discover.

Conversations, requirement gathering, and a clear understanding of the business — the users, the goals, the constraints. Nothing is built before it is understood.

Step 02

Design.

Wireframes and layouts in Figma. Type, colour, hierarchy — always testing on mobile first. The interface earns its keep before a single line of code is written.

Step 03

Build.

Clean, semantic HTML. Considered CSS. Purposeful JavaScript. Server logic where needed with PHP or Laravel, always paired with a well-shaped database.

Step 04

Deliver.

Cross-browser QA, performance tuning, SEO basics, and a friendly handover. I stay reachable after launch — good work is a relationship, not a transaction.
